# WorldCreator — World Roster

Candidate worlds for the gallery. Each is original content; the named games are
feel-references only — no IP is copied.

## 1. Hollowmere (working title) — IN PROGRESS, world #1

Whimsical British-fairytale action-RPG village. Witnessed-actions reputation,
gossip propagation, moral-fork quests, melee/ranged/magic weaving, day/night
village life, chickens. Feel-references: classic moral-choice fairytale RPGs.
Tone: warm, mischievous, darkly funny.

## 2. Neon Night Market

Compact cyberpunk market district: neon alleys, noodle stalls, robot vendors,
holo-signs (canvas textures), rain-slick reflections, steam vents. Hacking and
light stealth, credit economy. Feel-references: rainy cyberpunk city games.
Strength: atmosphere-dense, neon hides procedural seams.

## 3. Windward Isle

Small sunny open-world adventure island: beaches, cliffs, ruins, hilltop shrine,
animated ocean, climbing paths, treasure. Toon-stylized. Feel-references: cozy
exploration adventures. Strength: island naturally hides world bounds; most
likely to look polished.

## 4. The Greywick Academy

Magical-school courtyard hub: library, garden, tower, hidden basement, floating
candles and books, spell-casting, secret room quests. Feel-references: magic
school sims / social-hub RPGs. Strength: dense prop vocabulary suits procgen.

## 5. Hollowmere After Dark

Night/dark-fairytale variant of world #1: fog, lanterns, graveyard, eerie woods,
stranger villagers, harder moral choices. Reuses Hollowmere content with a
different lighting rig, palette, NPC roster, and quest set — cheapest world to
ship once #1 exists; proves the engine's theming flexibility.

## Build order

1 → hub polish → 2 (Neon Night Market) → 3 or 5 depending on appetite.
